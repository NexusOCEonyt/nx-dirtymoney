# nx-dirtymoney
Prodigy RP inspired dirty money system, you get 10 folded cash, roll it into a roll, with 10 rolls you can "Organize it" in to a band of cash

ox-inventory dependant

you need these items in your items.lua
folded_cash
black_money
cash_band

folded_cash - Folded cash
black_money - CASH ROLL
cash_band - Band of cash
